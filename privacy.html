Privacy Policy

This policy outlines how Budget Trails collects, uses, and protects your information. Last updated: 2025-10-18.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to improve our services:

  • Analytics: We use privacy-focused analytics to understand traffic patterns without tracking individual users. No personal data is stored.
  • Budget Calculator: Data entered into the budget calculator is processed in your browser and is not stored on our servers.
  • Contact: If you contact us via email, we will use your email address to respond to your inquiry.

How We Use Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Improve website content and functionality.
  • Respond to your questions or feedback.
  • Ensure the security of our website.

Cookies and Local Storage

We use `localStorage` to save your preferences, such as the selected tab on budget tables. This is stored in your browser and is not sent to our servers. We do not use tracking cookies.

Your Rights

You have the right to know what information we hold about you and to request its deletion. Since we do not store personal data from general site usage, there is typically no data to provide or delete. For inquiries, please contact us.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at hello@budgettrails.au.
